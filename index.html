John Luther Rogers

TANA Education

Recognized Speaker and Lecturer on
China and International Business

International businessman, attorney, strategist and educator John Rogers has spent the past 30 years commuting between his Chicago home base and Asia. The seasoned businessman has advised US domestic and Chinese companies on their international expansion strategies , established manufacturing operations around the world, and knows the needs of the factory as well as the boardroom and finance.
Rogers is frequently asked to address issues regarding international corporate governance and business ethics and his numerous speaking engagements include major universities, professional and trade organizations. Working at the intersection of law and business, he shares his knowledge via faculty appointments at prestigious universities.
He currently resides in Beijing.
